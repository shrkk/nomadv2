# Phase 03.3: City Detail View & Photo Gallery - Context

**Gathered:** 2026-04-09
**Status:** Ready for planning

<domain>
## Phase Boundary

Enhance the existing CountryDetailSheet so that tapping a city card transitions the sheet into a city-focused view with an expanded multi-photo carousel (all photos for that city), stats pill, and trip logs — all within the same sheet, with full-page horizontal swipe to navigate between cities in the country.

</domain>

<decisions>
## Implementation Decisions

### City Detail Layout
- **D-01:** Tapping a city card in the city strip transitions the CountryDetailSheet into a city-focused view — no new sheet or navigation destination
- **D-02:** The expanded carousel shows ALL photos for the selected city (not just 1 representative photo) — swipeable full-width paged carousel
- **D-03:** Below the photo carousel: stats pill row + chronological trip log cards (same pattern as country detail)
- **D-04:** Country name (e.g. "United States") remains as the top-left header throughout the city view

### Photo Gallery Behavior
- **D-05:** Photos in the carousel are view-only — no tap interaction, no full-screen viewer, no zoom
- **D-06:** Photo loading strategy is Claude's discretion (lazy load vs upfront — optimize for performance and memory)

### Navigation & Transitions
- **D-07:** City strip (thumbnail row) stays visible at top when in city-focused view — tapping another city card also navigates to it
- **D-08:** Swiping left/right on the edges navigates to the next/previous city — full page swipe (entire sheet content including city strip highlighting swipes as one unit)
- **D-09:** The city strip selection indicator syncs with the swipe position — swiping to Portland highlights Portland in the strip

### City Stats & Content
- **D-10:** Stats pill shows the same 3 stats as country level (trips · km · photos) but scoped to the selected city
- **D-11:** Trip logs section shows trips for the selected city in chronological order, using existing TripLogCard component

### Claude's Discretion
- Photo loading/caching strategy (lazy vs batch, memory management)
- Transition animation between country overview and city-focused view
- Page indicator style (dots, or rely on city strip as indicator)
- Empty states for cities with no photos
- Photo sort order within the carousel (chronological assumed)
- Swipe gesture handling (UIPageViewController-style vs TabView paging)

</decisions>
